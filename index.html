The True Story

Seven stolen. One refused to leave anyone behind.

In Changchun, Jilin province, China, seven neighbourhood dogs were stolen by traffickers bound for a dog meat shop. Loaded onto a moving truck on the Changshuang Expressway — they escaped.

Instead of scattering, they formed a tight formation. Larger dogs circled an injured German Shepherd. And at the front, looking back every few steps to count his companions — a small, stubborn Corgi. His name: Da Pang. The Leader.

For two days they crossed highways and open fields. They ignored everyone who tried to redirect them. 17 kilometres later, all seven walked back into their village. 230 million people watched. The world wept.

Da Pang didn't run faster. He ran at the speed of his slowest friend.
